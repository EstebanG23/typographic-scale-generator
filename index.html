Headlines & Subtitles
In the type scale, headlines span from a range of 1 through 6. Headlines are the largest text on the screen, reserved for short, important text or numerals.

For headlines, you can choose an expressive font, such as a display, handwritten, or script style. These unconventionals font designs have details and intricacy that help attract the eye.

Body & Captions
In the type scale, Body, captions, & overlines are used to display the majority of information in a screen, and have to be readable at small sizes. We recommend using a font that has multiple weights, and works well at small sizes.

Body text comes in ranges 1-2, and it’s typically used for long-form writing as it works well for small text sizes. For longer sections of text, a serif or sans serif typeface is recommended.
